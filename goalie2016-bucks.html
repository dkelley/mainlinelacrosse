Greg Cattrano's
Co-Ed Lacrosse Goalie Clinic

Sunday January 24th, 2016

12pm-3pm

For the 15th consecutive year former NCAA, MLL and NLL star goalie, Greg Cattrano, will be holding his individual goalie training at The Shipley School in Bryn, Mawr, PA. Info below. Space limited to the first 30 goalies!

  • Group Work

  • Tips, Pointers
