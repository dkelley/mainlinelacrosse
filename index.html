Main Line Lacrosse

Main Line Lacrosse is proud to be offering a specialized Lacrosse opportunities for boys & girls Ages 5 to 16. We work with expert coaches from NCAAs top programs and top players to bring great lacrosse options to the Main line!

Our Philosophy

We believe learning the game should be fun, and that while playing games is an important part of development taking the time to develop the core skills required is a fundemantal part of the process.
